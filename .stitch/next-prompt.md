Create a loyalty and membership page for PetShop shoppers that explains perks, member pricing, recurring savings, and trust-building benefits.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, desktop-first responsive ecommerce
- Theme: Light, flat, minimal, friendly, modern retail experience
- Background: Cloud White (#F8FBFF) with Soft Sky (#EAF3FB) alternating section bands
- Primary Accent: PetShop Blue (#306BAC) for trust, structure, and hero emphasis
- CTA Accent: Sunny Orange (#FF8C42) for Shop Now, Add to Cart, and urgent offers
- Supporting Accent: Mint Pop (#6ED3CF) for friendly merchandising details
- Text Primary: Ink Navy (#16324F), body text Slate Gray (#5B6B7A)
- Font: Poppins for headings, Inter for body text
- Components: Equal-height flat cards, rounded buttons, rounded inputs, subtle hover scale and shadow, clean iconography
- Layout: Wide aligned sections, centered container, 3-4 column product grids, 6-card category grid, strong CTA hierarchy, clean footer

**PAGE STRUCTURE:**
1. Hero section introducing PetShop membership with bold benefits and primary CTA
2. Benefits grid covering free shipping, early access, recurring savings, and exclusive bundles
3. Pricing comparison between guest checkout and member pricing
4. Testimonials or proof section from loyal pet owners
5. FAQ and reassurance section about billing flexibility and cancellation
6. Strong footer CTA to join membership
