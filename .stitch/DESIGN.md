# Design System: PetShop
**Project ID:** `13643797305037033191`

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
PetShop uses a clean flat ecommerce visual language with friendly shapes, clear merchandising hierarchy, and upbeat promotional energy. The interface should feel trustworthy enough for checkout, warm enough for pet lovers, and structured enough for fast browsing. Sections are airy and easy to scan, with bold banners, colorful category shortcuts, and consistently elevated CTA surfaces.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
- **PetShop Blue** (`#306BAC`) - Primary brand color for hero backgrounds, navigation emphasis, key accents, and trust cues.
- **Sunny Orange** (`#FF8C42`) - Primary call-to-action color for purchase buttons, promotional highlights, and urgency states.
- **Cloud White** (`#F8FBFF`) - Main page background to keep the store feeling fresh and spacious.
- **Soft Sky** (`#EAF3FB`) - Secondary section background for promo bands and content separation.
- **Ink Navy** (`#16324F`) - Primary heading and strong text color.
- **Slate Gray** (`#5B6B7A`) - Body text and supporting product metadata.
- **Mint Pop** (`#6ED3CF`) - Supporting accent for badges, category icons, and light merchandising details.

## 3. Typography Rules
Use Poppins for all headings, price emphasis, section labels, and CTA text. Headings should feel rounded, confident, and prominent, with large hero type and crisp merchandising hierarchy. Use Inter for all paragraph text, filters, metadata, and form labels. Preserve strong contrast and compact readable line lengths. Large promotional moments can use bold weights, while supporting copy should stay clean and moderate.

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Buttons:** Flat, rounded rectangles with generous horizontal padding, bold label text, and strong color contrast. Primary actions use Sunny Orange. Secondary actions use white or Soft Sky backgrounds with blue outlines.
* **Cards:** Equal-height product and category cards with soft corners, flat fills, light shadow, and subtle hover lift. Product cards should support badges, star ratings, and compact purchase CTA placement.
* **Inputs:** Spacious search and newsletter inputs with rounded corners, white fill, clear borders, and calm blue focus states.
* **Badges:** Compact rounded pills for discounts, ratings, and shipping benefits.
* **Navigation:** Clean horizontal header with central search, concise links, and right-aligned commerce utilities.

## 5. Layout Principles
Desktop-first responsive ecommerce layout with clear section rhythm, consistent side gutters, and card-based merchandising grids. Use 3-4 column product layouts, 6-up icon category grids, wide promotional bands, and modular content blocks that can collapse cleanly for smaller screens. Maintain strong alignment, equal heights, and obvious CTA paths.
